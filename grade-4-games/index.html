Welcome to our fourth grade games page! We’ve made it our mission to make learning fun and engaging.

On this page, you’ll discover a wide range of games that include math, puzzles, typing, logic and more. These games are specially crafted to provide an interactive and fun learning experience for kids.

Here are just a few of our favorite games that we think you’ll love:

Maze: Put your skills to the test in this challenging puzzle game. Draw a path through the maze and race against the clock as you navigate through different difficulty levels.

Photon Path: Take on the role of an engineer in this cosmic adventure game. Direct a ray of light through an abstract world and master each challenge as you level up.

Word Search: Test your vocabulary skills with this classic word search game. Choose from different languages and categories of words to find, and aim to find them all as quickly as possible to earn the highest score.

One Move: Can you solve each puzzle with just one move? Pick a puzzle block and slide it to a new position, and as the blocks fill an entire row, the row will be cleared. Clear each board to level up!

Elephant Feed: It’s a peanut frenzy in this fun language arts game! Perfect your skills at matching synonyms as your elephant eats lots of peanuts. Do your best to match the words correctly as quickly as possible to win!

Penguin Jump Multiplication: It’s ice cold, but the penguins love jumping over the frozen lake. In this multiplayer math game, hop to it as you answer multiplication facts with other students. Jump from one ice block to the next and win the game!

So, what are you waiting for? Dive in and discover the exciting and engaging games we have waiting for you on our fourth grade game page! Happy gaming!
